Your party.
Everyone's playlist.

MusiQ is a real-time music video jukebox that lets everyone at a gathering search YouTube and add their favourite songs to a shared playlist.

How it works

search

Search Songs

Search millions of music videos on YouTube directly from the app and add them to the shared playlist.

share

Share & Invite

Share a simple link so anyone at the party can add songs from their own device — no app install needed.

sync

Real-Time Sync

The playlist updates in real-time for everyone. Reorder songs, skip tracks, and control playback live.
